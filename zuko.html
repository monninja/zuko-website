Zuko : Fire Prince

About Zuko

Zuko is banished prince of the fire nation. His father, fire lord Ozai banished him for speaking out of turn during a fire nation meeting. Zuko needs to restore his honor the fire lord tasked to finid the Avatar who's been missing for 100 years. Zuko begins his hunt for Avatar but he finds himself conflicted his internal compass. Is he good or bad? As his journery gets more difficult, his questions is answered. He is good and his personla legend is to restore the honor of the fire nation by apologizing to the other nations and undoing all thier actions for the next 100 years.
